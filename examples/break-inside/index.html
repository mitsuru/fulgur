break-inside

A block with break-inside: avoid is never split across a page boundary. If it would straddle the bottom of a page, fulgur promotes the whole block to the next page instead.

This example places several paragraphs near the bottom of page one, then introduces a callout box that would not fit in the remaining space. Without break-inside: avoid the box would be sliced mid-content; with it, the box is kept whole and appears at the top of page two.

Key Insight — Page-breaking algorithms in PDF generators commonly split figures, tables, and callout boxes mid-content. break-inside: avoid instructs fulgur's pagination engine to promote the entire block to the following page rather than allowing a split. This is especially useful for figures with captions, multi-row tables, and styled boxes where a mid-break would destroy the visual grouping.
