## 7. Signing Procedure

Unlike the Individual CLA, this Corporate Agreement is **not** signed via a bot
comment on a pull request. Corporate signing is completed via the following
out-of-band process:

1. Complete Schedule A below with Your initial list of Designated Contributors.
2. Fill in the signature block with Your entity details.
3. An authorized representative signs (digital signature in PDF, or physical
   signature scanned), along with the authorized representative's printed name,
   title, and date.
4. Coordinate the submission with the maintainer by opening a
   [GitHub issue](https://github.com/fulgur-rs/fulgur/issues/new) tagged `cla`.
   The maintainer will respond with the canonical submission channel for Your
   signed PDF, which will be one of:
   - A pull request adding the signed PDF to
     `.github/corporate-signatures/<company-slug>.pdf`, or
   - A dedicated email address provided in the issue thread for jurisdictions
     or companies that cannot publish a signed PDF in a public repository.
5. Upon acceptance, Lutom LLC. will countersign and record Your Agreement in
   the public signatory list.

To update Schedule A after signing, submit a written notice (via GitHub issue
or pull request) signed by a person with authority to bind You. Updates take
effect upon acknowledgment by Lutom LLC.
